Resume

Summary

After five years of teaching, I am transitioning out of the classroom and into policy work. This is a significant change, but I am always ready for a challenge.

My desire to change careers solidified through my personal project of tracking COVID-19 cases and mitigation efforts in DC Public Schools. After hearing frustration from friends and colleagues about the scarcity of accessible information from city agencies, I decided to find and share the information myself. In doing so, I realized that I loved breaking down data to bring out the stories hidden within and ultimately share the information in a way that is comprehensive, comprehensible, and most importantly, actionable. I also discovered an interest in making the government work effectively for its constituents. I continue to update and track metrics daily derived from six different government agencies including the almost 8,000 COVID cases reported in DCPS in the 2021-2022 school year.

Through my professional experience, education, and personal projects, I’ve cultivated communicative and analytical skills that would transition naturally to this new career path. I also engage in continuous learning every day to gain additional skills and knowledge that will help me make the leap between my previous profession and my next opportunity. Now, I'm looking for an organization that can use my technical mindset, passion for transparent and accountable government, and experience in education policy and practice to advance equity-minded work.
